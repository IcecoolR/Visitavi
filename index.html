Visitavi 🌍

Visitavi is your personal travel companion that helps you track and visualize your journeys around the world. Whether you're a seasoned globetrotter or just starting your travel adventures, Visitavi makes it easy to mark the countries you've explored and plan your future destinations.

How to Use 🗺️

Click on any country on the interactive map to cycle through three states:
• Gray: Countries you haven't visited yet
• Yellow: Countries on your wishlist
• Green: Countries you've visited

Features ✨

• Interactive world map with smooth zoom and pan
• Real-time statistics tracking your travel progress
• Visual legend to understand country status at a glance
• Responsive design that works on all devices
• Data persistence - your travel data is saved locally

Travel Statistics 🌟

Keep track of your global exploration with detailed statistics including the number of countries visited, countries on your wishlist, and your overall world exploration percentage.

Start Your Journey 🎯

Ready to start mapping your adventures? Switch to the Map View and begin clicking on countries to mark your travels and build your personal travel story!
